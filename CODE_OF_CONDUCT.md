# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project is a collaborative space. Everyone participating — contributors, maintainers, and users — is expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and receive constructive feedback graciously.
- Assume good intent; ask for clarification before assuming malice.
- Focus on the work and ideas, not the person.
- Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels.

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks or sustained negative commentary directed at individuals.
- Sharing private information about others without their consent.
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider unprofessional or unwelcome.

## Scope

These standards apply in all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where this project is represented.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/drukmano/quent/issues) or by contacting the maintainer through their [GitHub profile](https://github.com/drukmano). Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
